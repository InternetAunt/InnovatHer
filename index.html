About Us

InnovateHer is on a mission to empower girls from underserved communities by equipping them with essential tech skills. We envision a world where every girl has the confidence, knowledge, and creativity to lead in technology.

Our Vision

To create a future where every girl, regardless of her background, has the opportunity to explore, learn, and thrive in the field of technology. We strive to bridge the gender gap in tech and inspire the next generation of female leaders and innovators.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality tech education and mentorship to girls in underserved communities. Through workshops, coding camps, and continuous support, we aim to build a community of empowered young women ready to make a mark in the tech industry.

Volunteer

Join our passionate team of volunteers and help make a lasting impact in the lives of young girls. We are looking for tech experts, mentors, educators, and administrative support to bring our vision to life.
